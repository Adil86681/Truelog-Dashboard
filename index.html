🎫 TrueLog Ticket Dashboard Bookmarklet

A one-click live dashboard that overlays all your tickets on TrueLog — with a built-in detail panel so you can preview any ticket without leaving the dashboard.

1

Drag to your bookmarks bar

Drag the button below into your Chrome bookmarks bar. If you don't see it, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac).

Drag this into your bookmarks bar

🎫 TrueLog Tickets
Can't drag? Right-click the button, choose "Bookmark this link", and save it to your Bookmarks Bar folder.
2

Go to the TrueLog tickets page

Navigate to inventory.truelog.com.sg/tickets/sf while logged in. The bookmarklet reads the allTickets data the page already loads into memory — all 2,834+ tickets.

3

Click the bookmark — then click any ticket

The dashboard opens over the page. Click any row to open a detail panel on the right showing status, tracking info, SLA, assignee and more. Hit "Load Full Details" to also pull in the description and comments.
