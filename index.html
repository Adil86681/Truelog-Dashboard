TrueLog Ticket Dashboard Bookmarklet

A one-click live dashboard that overlays all your tickets on TrueLog. No server, no CORS issues — it reads the data the page already loads (all 2,834+ tickets).

1

Drag to your bookmarks bar

Drag the button below into your Chrome bookmarks bar. If you don't see it, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to show it.

Drag this into your bookmarks bar

🎫 TrueLog Tickets
Can't drag? Right-click the button above, choose "Bookmark this link", and save it to your Bookmarks Bar folder.
2

Go to the TrueLog tickets page

Navigate to inventory.truelog.com.sg/tickets/sf and make sure you're logged in. The bookmarklet only works from that page — it reads the ticket data the page already loads into memory.

3

Click the bookmark

Click 🎫 TrueLog Tickets in your bookmarks bar. The dashboard opens instantly over the page. Click the bookmark again (or the ✕ Close button) to dismiss it.
